## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard built in **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze sales and outlet performance data for Blinkit – India’s last-minute delivery app.

The dashboard converts raw transactional data into meaningful insights to support revenue optimization and outlet expansion strategies.

---

## 📌 Key Metrics

- **Total Sales:** $1.20M  
- **Number of Items:** 8,523  
- **Average Sales per Item:** $141  
- **Average Rating:** 3.9  

---

## 🔎 Business Problem

The objective was to analyze:
- Which outlet tiers generate the highest revenue?
- Which outlet sizes and types perform best?
- Which product categories drive maximum sales?
- How sales vary by fat content and location tier?

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### 🏬 Outlet Performance
- Tier 3 outlets generated the highest revenue (~$472K).
- Supermarket Type 1 contributed the largest share of total sales (~$787K).
- Medium-sized outlets outperformed small and high-sized outlets.

### 🛍 Product Performance
- Top-selling categories include Fruits, Snacks, and Household items.
- Low Fat products contributed significantly to total revenue.

### 📍 Location & Establishment Trends
- Sales trend analysis based on outlet establishment year.
- Revenue comparison across Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 locations.
